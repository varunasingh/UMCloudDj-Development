How do I get my course onto the mobile device?
Android:
  1. Make sure the app is installed - install Ustad Mobile from Google Play app store. Open the app on your phone
  2. in eXeLearning click file -> Export -> Ustad Mobile. Copy that folder from your computer to the phone - connect your phone with a USB cable and find the folder ustadmobileContent - paste the exported folder in
  3. If needed on the app click the rescan button and your course will be found and can be opened
Feature Phones (Java 2 Micro Edition)
  1. Make sure the app is installed - visit http://i.ustadmobile.com and install it via the phone browser or download and use your phone's app installer (e.g. Nokia PC Suite)
  2. Put an SD memory card into your computer and make a folder called ustadmobileContent . Put the exported folder in the ustadmobileContent folder
If you need more help go to www.ustadmobile.com.
